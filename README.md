# LearnMate AI

> Building an AI-powered learning companion that helps students learn smarter, stay consistent, and track their complete learning journey.

---

## 🎯 Why LearnMate AI?

Students today use different apps for different purposes:

- Google Keep for notes
- Notion for planning
- YouTube for learning
- ChatGPT for doubts
- Google Calendar for scheduling
- Excel to track progress

Everything is scattered.

**LearnMate AI aims to bring all of this into a single platform.**

Instead of being another to-do app, it is designed to become an intelligent learning companion that understands what you're learning, tracks your progress, and eventually recommends what to study next using AI.

---

## 🚀 Vision

The long-term vision is to build a platform where a student can:

- Organize subjects and learning topics
- Create daily study plans
- Track consistency and progress
- Receive AI-powered study recommendations
- Identify weak concepts automatically
- Build personalized learning roadmaps

The goal is to make learning structured, measurable, and personalized.
